About me

I grew up in Texas, for the most part, and went to school in a small town. It was definitely Texas; my high school had a rodeo team to go along with the usual sports.

I moved out to Los Angeles because I wanted to write movies. And I did; I wrote a bunch of them, actually. The only problem was that no one wanted to pay me for them.

Eventually I got tired of working at an unfulfilling day job, and needed to make a change. I think writing screenplays and writing code are similar in many ways. You need to rely on logic, planning, and you need to know how to get from point A to point G.

Also, there's a lot of pounding your head against the wall in both fields.

Right now I live in Los Angeles with my wife and our two dogs. I like cooking, poker, golf, and hiking, and I also have a pretty good sense of humor. I mean, if this whole thing doesn't have you doubled over in laughter, I don't know what to tell you.
